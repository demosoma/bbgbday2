Today the universe is exactly as old as it needs to be for you to be here.

That's not a small thing.

The whole wild machinery of time — every star that had to burn, every choice that had to be made, every coincidence that had to land just right — all of it conspired to put you in this moment, reading this, breathing.

I hope this birthday feels like something.

Not necessarily big. Not necessarily loud. But *something*. A warmth. A sense that you are known and seen and loved by more than you can always measure.

Because you are.

Happy birthday, Babygirl.

The best is still ahead.

*— Always*
