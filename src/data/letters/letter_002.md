I don't know if you believe in moments.

Not events — moments. The ones that fit inside a second but somehow take up the rest of your life.

The first time I really *saw* you, I didn't know I was storing it away. But I was. The way you laughed at something I hadn't heard. The way you moved like you weren't trying to be anything in particular.

I think about that a lot.

How ordinary it looked from the outside. How different it felt from inside.

That was the moment, if you want to call it something. That was when everything quietly became before and after.

*Thank you for existing at the same time as me.*
