If you're reading this, you made it all the way.

Through every year, every chapter, every quiet in-between moment that never made it into a photograph but somehow carved itself into who you are — you made it here.

And I want to say something I never quite found the words for:

You were never ordinary to me.

Not on the days you thought you were. Not on the days you tried to be invisible. Not on the days you were certain the world wasn't watching.

I was always watching.

Not in a way that counted anything against you. In a way that noticed the details — the way you laugh too loud at something that actually got you, the way you go quiet when you care about something, the way you carry other people's feelings like they belong to you.

That's rare.

You are rare.

I don't know what the future holds. I'm not sure anyone does. But I know this:

Whatever it brings, I want to be there for it. The small Tuesdays. The big Octobers. The strange, unmemorable Thursdays that somehow turn out to be everything.

Happy Birthday.

The universe waited a very long time to make someone exactly like you.

It was worth every moment.

*— Always yours*
